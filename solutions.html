E-Visits

E-visits bring convenience to patients.

For many patients, it's often hard to find time in their busy lives to schedule and attend a doctor's appointment. Going to a doctor's office is simply inconvenient for many, and this leads to many preventable conditions worsening. This is where virdoc can help. Our e-visit platform will allow your patients to a reach a doctor quickly and efficiently.

Here's what you can do with VirDoc's e-visit platform.
  • On-demand medical appointments complete with a virtual waiting room
  • View test results and prescriptions online
  • Online appointment scheduling

COVID-19

Monitor your COVID-19 patients as they recover at home.

It's important to monitor your COVID-19 patients at home to ensure that their condition is improving rather than worsening. VirDoc provides your hospital with the necssary software tools to monitor your patients while they recover from COVID-19.

Here's what you can do with VirDoc's COVID-19 monitoring platform.
  • Track patient temperatures
  • Monitor patient feedback about their illness
  • Track the progress of the virus
