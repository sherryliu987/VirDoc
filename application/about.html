Our History

  1. 1948

    First radiological images sent via telephone

  2. 1960s

    Nebraska Psychiatry Institute uses closed-circuit TV for psychiatric consultations

  3. 1961 U.S. Space Program Conducts Test Flights with Animals Using Remote Medical Monitoring Systems
  4. 1990s The Internet is Born
  5. 2010-16 More than 11,000 papers using keywords "telemedicine" and "telehealth" published
  6. 1993 Founding of the American Telemedicine Association
  7. 2020 COVID-19 pandemic spurs rapid expansion of telehealth use to reduce physical contact
  8. 2021 Virdoc is founded in an effort to provide affordable, inexpensive, accessible, and high quality care to all
